Our Story

BORN ON THE
STREETS.

We started with one idea: Mexican street food made properly. Not chain-restaurant Mexican. Not tex-mex. Real, fresh, fiery food — the kind you’d find on a market stall in Oaxaca, built from scratch in Sheffield.

Over 15 years, Sheffield has made us their own. We’ve stayed family-run, stayed obsessed with sourcing, and stayed honest about what goes in your burrito. Everything is prepared fresh every morning — salsas, guacamole, marinades, all of it.

We work with Moss Valley Fine Meats, Our Cow Molly, and Yee Kwan — Sheffield producers we’ve trusted for over a decade.
