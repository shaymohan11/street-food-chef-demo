Our Story

BORN ON THE
STREETS.

We started with a simple idea: Mexican street food made properly. Not chain-restaurant Mexican. Not tex-mex. Real, fresh, fiery food — the kind you'd find on a market stall in Oaxaca.

Over 15 years, Sheffield has made us their own. We've stayed family-run, stayed obsessed with sourcing, and stayed honest about what we put in your burrito. Everything is prepared fresh daily — salsas, guacamole, marinades. All of it.

We work with Moss Valley Fine Meats, Our Cow Molly, and Yee Kwan — Sheffield producers we trust.
